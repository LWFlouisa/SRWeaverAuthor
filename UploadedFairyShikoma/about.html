ABOUT UPLOADED FAIRY

Uploaded Fairy is a graphic novel told in the form of a comic strip, that explores the themes of love and justice. And Hemato-Tomato choice between letting Ehena-Marie be executed, or running off with her to another part of the US. Here, we see a glipse of the United States before it was taken over by the French and Japanese governments as a side war during the era of world war III. In this alternative timeline, Marine Le Pen won the French election, and Shinzo Abe was still the prime minister of Japan.

ABOUT S.R. WEAVER
